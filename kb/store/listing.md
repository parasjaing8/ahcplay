## Full description (<=4000 chars)

AHC Player turns your Android TV or Fire TV into a private streaming box for
the media you already own — no subscriptions, no uploads, no third-party
cloud.

WHERE YOUR MEDIA LIVES
• AiHomeCloud NAS — pair with your AiHomeCloud box over your home network
  (QR pairing, per-profile PINs)
• SMB shares — browse any network share on your LAN
• Internal storage & USB drives — play files stored directly on the device

A REAL HOME SCREEN, NOT A FILE BROWSER
• Posters, backdrops, and genres pulled automatically from The Movie Database
  (TMDB) for everything in your library
• "Continue Watching" row picks up exactly where you left off
• Movie/show counts and a rotating backdrop collage on the home screen
• Multiple profiles, each with their own watch history and optional PIN

BUILT FOR THE REMOTE, NOT THE MOUSE
• Every screen is D-pad first: focus highlights, predictable navigation,
  no accidental taps
• On-screen keyboard only opens when you actually need to type

A PLAYER THAT JUST PLAYS
• Powered by libVLC — wide format support (MKV, MP4, AVI, and more),
  multiple audio tracks and subtitle tracks, fast seeking
• Resume playback automatically from where you stopped

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
• No ads, no analytics, no account required
• Your library, watch history, and login tokens never leave your device or
  your own NAS
• Connections to your AiHomeCloud NAS are certificate-pinned

REQUIREMENTS
• Android TV or Fire TV device
• A home network with an AiHomeCloud NAS, an SMB share, or local/USB storage
  containing your media files
